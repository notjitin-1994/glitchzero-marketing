01

What's New

The redesigned website represents a complete transformation—from a product-focused storefront to a premium B2B marketing platform that positions S-TEX as India's premier luxury performance textile manufacturer.

Brand Positioning

  • New visual identity reflecting "Where Heritage Meets Performance"
  • Premium design language aligned with global leaders like Sunbrella and Perennials
  • Clear messaging for architects, interior designers, and hospitality professionals

Site Structure

  • 8 primary pages with 13 dynamic routes covering all brand messaging
  • Dedicated pages for 6 textile collections with galleries, features, and applications
  • 6 industry application pages targeting hospitality, residential, marine, outdoor, corporate, and shade solutions
  • Comprehensive "Why S-TEX" page showcasing your competitive advantages

Key Highlights

Pioneer Credentials: "First in India" badges prominently displayed for Jacquard Blackouts, Outdoor Performance fabrics, and 100% PU Leatherettes
Global Trust Signals: Partner showcase featuring Crate and Barrel, Pottery Barn, West Elm, Anthropologie, and Dillard's
Heritage Storytelling: Company timeline from 2001 to present, founder section, and manufacturing excellence narrative

Design & Experience

  • Full-viewport hero with sophisticated entrance animations
  • Smooth scroll behaviour throughout
  • Mobile-responsive design across all devices
  • Premium micro-interactions and scroll-triggered reveals

B2B Lead Generation

  • Multi-field contact form with professional role targeting
  • Project type categorisation (hospitality, residential, commercial)
  • Contact preference options
  • Integrated email delivery system

Technical Foundation

  • Built on Next.js 15 with modern React architecture
  • Optimised for fast loading (Core Web Vitals targets)
  • SEO-ready with structured data
  • Accessibility compliant (WCAG 2.1 AA)

02

What's Been Removed

As discussed, all e-commerce functionality has been eliminated:

✕ No product catalogue with pricing
✕ No shopping cart or checkout
✕ No user accounts
✓ Pure B2B inquiry focus
